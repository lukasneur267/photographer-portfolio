A documentary eye with an editorial finish.

My photographs have been selected and published by some of the most respected international magazines dedicated to weddings, fashion, and the visual arts.

Vogue, Elle Spose, The Real Wedding, and many other publications in Vienna and abroad have featured my work, recognizing its style, elegance, and narrative power. Each publication is an honor, but above all a confirmation that the truth of a gaze, the delicacy of an emotion, and the light of an authentic moment can become a universal image.
